Chopin's last published piece and only mature chamber work is a G minor sonata for this instrument dedicated to Auguste Franchomme. For 10 points each:


Name this instrument. The operetta composer Victor Herbert inspired Dvorak to write a concerto for this instrument in B minor.
Answer: cello [or violoncello]


Franchomme transcribed a piece in this genre by Chopin often nicknamed "Cello" for its heartsick left-hand melody. Chopin's other pieces in this didactic genre include ones nicknamed "Black Key" and "Revolutionary."
Answer: etude [accept study]


Franchomme and Chopin jointly wrote a Grand duo concertant for cello and piano based on themes from this Jewish composer's opera Robert le diable.
Answer: Giacomo Meyerbeer [or Jakob Liebmann Meyer Beer]
